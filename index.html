Our Story

Preserving tradition, one recipe at a time

Traditional Recipes, Modern Presentation

Founded in 2024, Rayalaseema Vari Ruchulu brings you the authentic taste of Rayalaseema cuisine with a contemporary twist. Our chefs use traditional cooking methods and the freshest ingredients to create dishes that honor our culinary heritage.

We source our spices directly from farmers in Andhra Pradesh to ensure the most authentic flavors in every dish. Our special Gongura leaves are grown in our own garden to maintain quality and freshness.

Whether you're craving our signature Natukodi Pulusu or our famous Rayalaseema Special Biryani, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that will transport you to the heart of Andhra Pradesh.
